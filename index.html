About Me

Originally from Georgia, I'm a 24 yrs old and am an aspiring Software Developer. Lover of dogs, food enthusiast, human and civil rights advocate. I've always been the tech savvy person of the family. I would always wander off and play with the cool gadgets and computers on display at Best Buy. My mom noticed this and said I should go into the technology field. Naturally of course, I went against what my mother suggested and pursued an education in health sciences. It wasn't until I graduated college, that I began questioning if I was on the right career path. I wanted something different and a new way to challenge myself. The Road to Hire program was brought to my attention and I jumped at the chance to create a new life for myself. Learning skills and languages I'd never thought possible, I found a new passion for technology and learning. Everyday I continue to learn and grow more confident in my skills and plan to complete other certifications, such as AWS. My primary goal for my career is to create a welcoming, easily accessible, and efficient experience not only for the client, but for my team as well. Eventually, I would love to use my work ethic, leadership and desire to cultivate community to work towards a position in management. I am confident in my capability to achieve this.
